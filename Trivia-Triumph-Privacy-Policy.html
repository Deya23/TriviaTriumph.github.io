The privacy of our visitors is our highest priority at Trivia Triumph. This Privacy Policy document explains the types of information that Trivia Triumph gathers, records, and uses.

Log Files

Trivia Triumph uses log files in accordance with standard protocol. When users use the software, these files record their usage. Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISPs), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and maybe click counts are among the data gathered by log files. These are not connected to any personally identifiable information. The information is used for trend analysis and app administration.The information will be used to perform trend analysis, app administration, user movement tracking, and demographic data collection.

Third Party Privacy Policies

The privacy policies of other advertisers and websites are not covered by Trivia Triumph's Privacy Policy. For more information, including how to opt-out of specific options, we recommend that you review the privacy policies of these third-party ad servers.

Children's Information

Our goal also includes providing additional security for kids when they use the internet. We advocate for parents and guardians to watch over, engage in, and/or supervise their children's internet activities.

Kids under the age of 13 do not voluntarily provide Trivia Triumph with any personally identifiable information. We highly advise you to get in touch with us right away if you believe your youngster submitted this kind of information on our app. We'll try our best to delete the information from our records as soon as possible.
